# Welcome!

I'm **Bhawuk**, and if you're reading this, you've landed in my "digital brain." 

Lately, I've been thinking a lot about the intersection of **automation and intelligence**. As an engineer focused on MLOps and DevOps, my day-to-day is usually spent architecting pipelines or debugging Kubernetes clusters. But this blog is where I strip away the logs and the YAML to talk about the *why* behind the work.

## What's Happening?

Right now, I'm deeply focused on:
- **Scalability**: Not just in terms of servers, but in terms of *efficiency*. How do we build systems that grow without becoming unmanageable?
- **ML Infrastructure**: Moving machine learning from a research project to a production-grade service that actually works at scale.
- **The Craft**: I believe engineering is as much an art as it is a science. The "cleanliness" of a CI/CD pipeline counts just as much as the code it deploys.

## My Philosophy

I don't just want to build things; I want to build things that **last**. Whether it's a small script or a multi-region cloud setup, my thinking is always: *How does this break? And how do we make it fail gracefully?*

I'm glad you're here to follow along. I'll be sharing my wins, my failures (the best teachers), and the occasional deep-dive into a new tool that has caught my eye.

**Hope you enjoy the journey.** If you have thoughts on distributed systems or just want to say hi, my [Contact](/contact) page is always open.

Stay building,
